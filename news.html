Conference 01

MANDATORY 2014 CONFERENCE ~ DUBLIN, IRELAND



The Global Freight Group has just enjoyed its 20th Anniversary conference in the beautiful city of Dublin. With The Four Seasons Hotel as its venue, quality was ensured.

The meeting of 100 participants from its global membership met for the GFG's annual conference from 19 - 21 O ctober 2014. With the location providing a stunning backdrop for business, members settled down to learn about developments within the GFG at the General Assembly, before their own face-to-face meetings during the following days.

With just under 1,400 one-to-one sales meetings, there was quite some business to be done!

The GFG introduced two new members to the Global Freight Group:
Greece: Grandair Ltd
Thailand: Leo Global Logistics Co., Ltd







Well known as the friendliest network you can join, our members proved the point by choosing to take a day to enjoy the Irish countryside together. With Kilkenny and Glendalough on the itinerary they received a stunning overview of Ireland's renowned countryside. Rain stopped long enough for them to play - exploring caves, castles and cloisters and follow the river alongside the monastic ruins.


To ensure you are involved in the next GFG gathering to enjoy all aspects of the friendly network at its best, don't delay in visiting our "Membership Opportunities" page to start your application today...






Conference 02

CONFERENCE NEWS - LOS ANGELES, USA - APRIL 2014




"The GFG was perhaps keeping work uppermost when it held its April 2014 conference literally opposite LAX airport, at the Embassy Suites LAX South...

GFG Group Administrator, Rachel Marchant, reports on the Los Angeles conference, 27 - 29 April 2014

The planes were taking off as was the GFG's 5th Los Angeles casual conference …

Having stayed at the Embassy Suites LAX South several times, it ahd been decided to try the Embassy Suites LAX South for a different view. Easy and quick transfers from airport to hotel afforded members the convenience of venue, even if LAX's main attractions were somewhat further away. There would be no distractions from the meetings!




IB&M and kindly offered some sponsorship towards the welcome party, sharing their products and services amongst the GFG members.




With 50% attendance at what is essentially an optional extra conference, there were plenty of companies with which to meet. However, to start off the meeting on a sociable note, the members met for a welcome party in style.

Left: Local host company, Ken Hamanaka's owners kindly invite all GFG members to a Mexican themed party.




Meetings are over time to learn about yourself...
Adding value to the meetings.

The GFG's friendly nature meant that this was not just a gathering of 30+ companies but a gathering of 45+ friends. Although the main motive is business, there were plentiful opportunities for socialising and strengthening the relationships for which the network is reknowned.
Left: GFG members enjoyed pizza at Rock and Brews Restaurant
